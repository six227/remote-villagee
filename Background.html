關於本遊戲 About the Game

《Game》是一款以香港都市傳說為背景的敘事驅動 RPG 遊戲。玩家將扮演一名遠足愛好者，在西貢遭遇詭異迷霧後，被神秘少年帶入一座不存在的村落——鎖羅盆村。在這裡，你將探索荒廢的村落、蒐集碎片化線索，並面對影響結局的關鍵選擇。遊戲融合香港在地傳說與懸疑氛圍，帶給玩家沉浸式的敘事體驗。

"Game" is a narrative-driven RPG set against the backdrop of Hong Kong's urban legends. Players take on the role of a hiking enthusiast who, after encountering a mysterious mist in Sai Kung, is led by a mysterious boy to a village that doesn't exist—So Lo Pun Village. Here, you will explore abandoned villages, collect fragmented clues, and face critical choices that determine the ending. The game blends local Hong Kong legends with an eerie atmosphere, offering players an immersive narrative experience.

📖 故事背景 Story Background

一次西貢遠足，一場詭異迷霧。隊友消失在傳說中的「西貢結界」，你被神秘少年帶往一座不存在的村落——鎖羅盆村。村中瀰漫著詭異的寂靜，時間彷彿停滯。你將在這裡尋找失散的隊友，追尋少年的秘密，並面對一個抉擇：離開，或留下。

A hiking trip in Sai Kung. A mysterious mist. Your teammates disappear into the legendary "Sai Kung Envelope," and you are led by a mysterious boy to a village that doesn't exist—So Lo Pun Village. An eerie silence fills the village, as if time has stopped. Here, you will search for your missing teammates, uncover the boy's secrets, and face a choice: leave, or stay.

✨ 特色內容 Features

• 香港在地傳說改編：取材自「西貢結界」、「鎖羅盆村」真實都市傳說
• 氛圍探索 × 環境敘事：在荒廢村落與山徑中尋找線索
• 多重結局：你的選擇將導向「離開」或「留下」
• 死亡與存檔機制：死亡返回上一個存檔點，可繼續遊玩

• Inspired by Hong Kong urban legends: Based on the real "Sai Kung Envelope" and "So Lo Pun Village"
• Atmospheric exploration × Environmental storytelling: Search for clues in abandoned villages and mountain trails
• Multiple endings: Your choices lead to "Leave" or "Stay"
• Death and save system: Death returns you to the last save point, allowing you to continue
